# Final Cleanup Prompt

Use this prompt before delivering the final CUMT thesis defense PPT.

## Checklist

- Thesis title exactly matches the PDF/user instruction.
- Slide count and structure match user scope.
- No fabricated thesis claims or experiment values.
- No copied reference-template thesis content.
- All images match their slide topics.
- Blurry or unreadable images are removed, cropped, enlarged, or replaced with native tables/diagrams.
- CUMT logo is consistent where required.
- Page titles: Heiti, 28-32 pt, deep blue, bold.
- Module titles: Heiti, 20-24 pt, deep blue, bold.
- Chinese body: Songti, 18-22 pt.
- Captions/table small text: Songti, 10-14 pt.
- English/numbers/acronyms/formulas: Times New Roman.
- PPO/experiment settings are in a readable three-line table when appropriate.
- Reward ablation and multi-scale slides use upper centered charts + lower concise conclusions.
- PNG previews exported.
- Visual report written.
- PPT opens successfully.
