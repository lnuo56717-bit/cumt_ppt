# Font Rules

Use these rules for China University of Mining and Technology undergraduate thesis defense PPTs.

## Page Titles

- Chinese font: 黑体 / SimHei
- Size: 28-32 pt
- Color: deep blue
- Weight: bold
- Format: `编号 + 中文标题`, such as `03 车间布局与物流路径`

## Module Titles

- Chinese font: 黑体 / SimHei
- Size: 20-24 pt
- Color: deep blue
- Weight: bold

## Body Text

- Chinese font: 宋体 / SimSun
- Size: 18-22 pt
- Color: black or dark gray
- Keep line spacing comfortable; avoid dense paragraphs.

## Captions And Table Small Text

- Chinese font: 宋体 / SimSun
- Size: 10-14 pt
- Do not use text below 10 pt unless it is a nonessential annotation.

## English, Numbers, Acronyms, Formulas

- Font: Times New Roman
- Applies to English words, numbers, formulas, Greek symbols, units, and acronyms.
- Examples: `Model-A`, `SVM`, `CNN`, `Python`, `Dataset-X`, `Tool-Y`, `60m×45m`, `2×10^5`, `1e-3`, `0.95`, `128`, `64`, `92.5`.

## Key Numbers

- Font: Times New Roman
- Size: 24-32 pt
- Color: red or deep blue
- Weight: bold
- Use for core experiment results and thesis defense takeaways.
